    Born on February 1, 2009, with a silver spoon in her mouth, Mars Merkaba Thedford has enjoyed a childhood that most can only dream of. Mars is the only daughter of renowned celebrities Erykah Badu and Jay Electronica. 

    Erykah Badu (mother)

    Her mother, Erykah Badu, born Erica Abi Wright, is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, and actress. Hailed as the “Queen of Neo-Soul,” Erykah Badu has released albums including Baduizm, Live, Mama’s Gun, Worldwide Underground, New Amerykah Part One, and New Amerykah Part Two.

    In 1998, Erykah Badu won the Grammy Award for Best R&B Album and Best Female R&B Vocal Performance. Similarly, she has acted in many TV shows and movies such as Blues Brothers 2000, The Cider House Rules, Say My Name, and House of D. 

    Jay Electronica (father)

    Mars Merkaba’s father is the well-known rapper and record producer Jay Electronica. Jay Electronica released his debut mixtape, Act I: Eternal Sunshine (The Pledge), in 2007. 

    Considered as one of the greatest lyricists of his time, Jay Electronica got signed to Jay-Z’s record label, Roc Nation, in 2010. The rapper has released two studio albums, including  A Written Testimony and Act II: The Patents of Nobility (The Turn). 

    Jay Electronica and Erica Badu’s relationship

    Jay Electronica and Erica Badu dated each other for five years. In September 2008, news about Erykah Badu’s pregnancy started doing the rounds after the Grammy Award-winning singer performed at the Austin City Limits festival and wore a figure-hugging smiley-face tee that clearly showed off her growing baby bump. 

    As the couple anticipated the arrival of their little one, Jay Electronica documented Erykah’s entire delivery process on Twitter. The rapper updated fans in real-time about Erykah’s contractions. 

    Likewise, he informed fans that their midwife was running a little late. Soon after, Jay announced that Erykah’s water had broken, and 20 minutes later, he announced the arrival of their daughter, Mars Merkaba Thedford. 

    Even though Erykah Badu and Jay Electronica are no longer together, the former couple continues to be on friendly terms and tries their best to be great co-parents to their daughter, Mars. In an interview, Erykah Badu dished on what it is like to raise kids in the world of entertainment. 

    She said,

    “We are a whole different generation than the generation [of entertainers] that left their children behind….We use our money to continue our lifestyle and to make sure our children are with us.”

    Educational Background

    While most parents prefer to send their children to school for their education, Erykah Badu has quite an unconventional approach to this matter. The “Bag Lady” singer once revealed on social media that all her children, including Mars Merkaba, are homeschooled. 

    Likewise, she said in a 2009 interview with People magazine,

    “All my children have been thru Badu school. They know the drill. The whole family gotta get involved.”

    She added,

    “I’ll handle the Physics, Math, Lit, Sociology, Health science, Astronomy, Astrology, Meditation, Religion, Economics, Art appreciation, Film making, Quantum Science, Molecular Physics… etc… ”

    She also mentioned that Jay Electronica teaches world/ planetary history and geography.

    Moreover, Badu talked about how her daughter Mars is being homeschooled and shared that her family helps little Mars “see the world through her own lens.” The Queen of Neo-Soul emphasized that she wants Mars to become her own person and that “she doesn’t need to become a mini Erykah or mini Jay.”
