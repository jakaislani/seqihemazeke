    It’s hard to discuss the best scream queens without discussing Felissa Rose. She is well recognized for her performance in the 1986 cult film Sleepaway Camp. The film established her as a horror genre legend, and she has since appeared in scores of scary films.

    Although you may know a lot about her there are still some things that you didn’t know about her. And we’ve discussed them below

    8 Things You Didn’t Know About Felissa Rose

    1. Dream Come True

    Felissa knew she wanted to be an actress from a young age, and she also enjoyed singing and dancing. She had ambitions to perform in Broadway musicals, but she later changed her mind. [1]

    2. Received Training From Newyork University

    Fans of horror films are already aware that Felissa was not welcomed back to the original Sleepaway Camp sequels. She was requested to reprise her role but declined in order to attend New York University and pursue professional acting training. [2]

    3. An Adventurous Person

    Felissa is the type of person that enjoys a good adrenaline rush and a fantastic adventure. She doesn’t back down from the excitement, whether she’s traveling or auditioning for a new part.

    4. Also Appeared In Theatres

    Scream queens aren’t typically thought of as serious actors, but Felissa is defying that prejudice. She is not only formally trained, but she also has theatre experience. She has been in various off-Broadway productions, including the play Phone Sex. [3]

    5. Proud Mom And Happy Wife

    She may be best known to the world as Angela, but away from the cameras, she is best known as a different type of star: a wife and mother. She has three children with musician Deron Miller, whom she has been married to since 2004.

    6. Producer

    Felissa’s popularity stems from her work in front of the camera, but it isn’t the only thing she is enthusiastic about. She also enjoys working behind the scenes and has done some production work throughout the years. [4]

    7. Loves To Socialize

    Felissa is the type of person who enjoys socializing and being around others, so meeting and interacting with her fans has always been one of her favorite activities.

    8. Enjoys Doing independent Projects

    Many performers avoid working on small/independent films since the money is usually low and there is little exposure. Felissa, on the other hand, enjoys working on her own projects. [5]
