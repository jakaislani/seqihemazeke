    Who Are Thomas Gabriel Siblings?: Born in 1973, Thomas Gabriel is an American singer and songwriter.

    Growing up, he spent time with grandparents Johnny Cash and June Carter Cash and became interested in music at an early age, taking up guitar at age 11.

    Who Are Thomas Gabriel Siblings?

    Thomas Gabriel has never shared any information regarding his siblings hence we can’t state whether or not he’s the only child of his parents. There are no records about that.

    Despite Gabriel’s early interest in music, Johnny Cash (his grandparent) steered him away from show business and into law enforcement.

    For eight years, he served as a police officer but allegedly resigned due to drinking and drug abuse. His addictions led to a lengthy arrest record and several years in prison.

    In prison, Gabriel turned his attention to music again. After his release, he continued to work on musical projects he had started while behind bars.

    Brian Oxley, a producer who purchased Johnny Cash’s farm in Tennessee and had taken an interest in the Cash family, persuaded Gabriel to enter rehab.

    After a year at the rehab, Gabriel began work with Brian Oxley on his first independent album, “Long Way Home”, released in 2018.

    In October 2018, 50 years after his grandfather’s live performance at Folsom State Prison in California, Gabriel performed two shows for prison inmates.

    In April 2020, Gabriel released the single “Right Side of the Dirt”. He called the song a reflection on how much his life has improved.

    On September 12, 2023, Gabriel made his Grand Ole Opry debut during a show to mark the 20th anniversary of his grandparent, Johnny Cash’s passing.

    Gabriel has been consistent throughout his career. His music has been described as a mix of Americana/Roots with a hint of country and rock.
