    Who is Vanessa Hudgens?

    Vanessa Hudgens is an American famous pop singer and actress born on 14th December 1988 (33 years old as of 2022). She was born in Salinas, California, the United States and she holds Us citizenship.

    Looking at her education, Hudgens attended Guajome Park Academy, California before she completed her graduation at Orange County School of the Arts.
    She started her career as a singer at a youth age.

    In 1998 Hudgens began to perform in musical theatre and has worked with various popular productions including; Carousel, The Wizard of Oz, The King and I, The Music Man, and Cinderella, After two years of working in various theatres as a singer, Hudgen started auditioning for commercials and television shows.

    She landed her first role in 2002 in an episode of the sitcom Still Standing, that ran on CBS. She rose to stardom in the year January 2006, after she portrayed Gabriella Montez, in one of the films titled High School Musical.

    The film “High School Musical” accumulate over 7.7 million viewers when it was premiered in the USA and became the most-watched film in 2006 in the USA.

    Since then, the pop singer has been featured in various popular film projects including; Sucker Punch, Thirteen, The Frozen Ground, Freaks of Nature, Sucker Punch, among others.

    Aside from acting, she has also released a lot of popular music projects including Breaking Free, which appeared at number 4 on the US Billboard Hot 100, V,(studio album), certified(studio album) gold, come back to me, and and “Say OK”.

    Moreover, she has also won several multiple awards Imagen Foundation Awards, Radio Disney Music Awards, Teen Choice Awards, Kids’ Choice Awards, Australian Kids’ Choice Awards and others.
