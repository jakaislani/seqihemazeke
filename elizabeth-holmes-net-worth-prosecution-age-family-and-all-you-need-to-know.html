    It will interest you to know who Elizabeth Holmes is and why she is so famous. Elizabeth is an American entrepreneur who was CEO of the medical diagnostic company Theranos Inc. The company was founded in the year 2003.

    She made name for herself by becoming the world’s youngest female billionaire at the age of 19. However, she was charged with fraud after 15 years.

    However, the billion-dollar business and its CEO, Elizabeth Holmes, ruled the world in 2014. Theranos was a ground-breaking concept created back then by a woman who was hailed as a genius and identified as a female Steve Jobs. Theranos was one of Silicon Valley’s unicorn firms, and Holmes was the youngest self-made female billionaire in the world.

    Then everything collapsed. Theranos’s technology’s flaws and errors were made public, as well as Holmes’s part in trying to hide everything. Theranos and Holmes were accused of committing significant fraud, and the business was forced to shut down its labs and testing facilities.
