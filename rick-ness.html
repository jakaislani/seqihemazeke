    American Reality show star, Rick Ness, is a star of the Discovery+ show Gold Rush. He is too damn serious about the mining thing, and it is apparent through his appearance on the show.

    Rick Ness started his television career in the year 2012 from the very show as part of Parker Schnabel’s crew. Still, people started noticing him, and it was only about time he came up with his independent operation.

    Rick Ness rolled out season 9 of the show Gold Rush with his own team, and he couldn’t be any serious about his work ever since. Here’s more to know about the reality TV Star.

    Wiki/Bio, Twin Brother

    Rick Ness was born in Escanaba, Michigan, to his parents Richard Ness and Judy Marie Bedard. The reality TV star has a twin brother, Randy Ness.

    Brothers aren’t identical, but two are close, as it seems on their social media presence. Rick never misses to wish his twin brother happy birthday; though small, the gesture suggests he is close to his brother.

    Band

    Believe it or not, Rick Ness was a musician before he started his mining career. The 40-year-old started playing the upright bass and even started his own band called “3.57 Stringband.”

    The reality TV star and his band were relatively successful and released three albums over the course of seven years. They even toured around the globe, which is pretty much impressive.
